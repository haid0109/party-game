Waiting for players...

Rules

Up to 7 players

The players will upload a soundfile each, which will then be added to the game

Each sound will be played once in a random order

The soundfile will be manipulated when played to be slower, faster etc...

The player will then have to guess what sound/music was just played from a mulitude of options

Once every soundfile has been played once the round ends, and result added to the highscore

They will then have a choice to begin another round or end the game
