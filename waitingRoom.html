Rules

Up to 7 players

The players will upload a soundfile each, which will then be added to the game

The soundfile will be edited to be slower, faster, reversed etc...

Each sound will be played once in a random order

The player will then have to guess what sound/music was just played

Once every soundfile has been played the round ends, and result added to the highscore

Players will then have a choice to begin another round or end the game
